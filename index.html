Weierstrass & Tauberian Theorems

REU · Summer 2024

A 28-page expository paper presenting Lebesgue's and Landau's proofs of the Weierstrass Approximation Theorem, the Stone-Weierstrass Theorem, and classical Tauberian results including Hardy-Littlewood. Written with Sourav Ghosh, Patrick Oglesby, and Clark Wu at Notre Dame.

Matchmaking Problems

Directed Reading Program · Spring 2026

A paper exploring mechanism design through the lens of auctions and the Perfect Marriage Problem. Covers the Vickrey Auction, Gale-Shapley Algorithm, and stability theory. Written through Budapest Semesters in Mathematics under Máté Simon.
